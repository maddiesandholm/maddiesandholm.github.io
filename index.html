Maddie Sandholm

is a print and digital designer based in Manhattan.

Maddie was the Managing Editor of The Fordham Observer for the 2022-23 school year and has four years of experience designing and supervising print layout through The Observer and The Fordham Undergraduate Research Journal.

Accessible and inclusive design is very important to them. They are looking for opportunities to center those practices and create or oversee the creative process in the publishing world.

Maddie designed this website with HTML and CSS, and it features the documentary she created for her Digital Production for New Media course, along with a few more pieces from her portfolio.
